Exterior HDR

Hello! My name is Phillip Smelser and I am a senior at Eastern Washington University. I am majoring in Technical Communication with a minor in Visual Communication Design. I am currently on track to graduate in June 2019.

I took this photograph of the Computer Engineering Building (CEB) at EWU as part of a project for DESN 350. I created the High Dynamic Range (HDR) image by using several images and combining them in Adobe Photoshop.

The HDR process is an effective method to capture a vivid image in low light conditions. The image I took of the CEB was taken at sunset and both the interior lights and exterior details can be seen.
